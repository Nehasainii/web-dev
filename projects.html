Kim Jongin

January 14,1994-present

South Korean singer,dancer and actor.

Kim jongin is a member of south korean boy group EXO and SUPER-M.He debuted as a soloist in November 2020 with the release of his extended-play, Kai.Kai was the first Exo member to be formally introduced to the public on December 23, 2011. Apart from his musical career, Kai has also starred in various television dramas such as Choco Bank (2016) , Andante (2017), and Spring Has Come (2018). Kai is widely considered to be one of the best dancers of South Korea and K-pop.In December 2017, Kai was chosen as the cover model for December's issue of The Big Issue , a magazine known for helping the homeless. The magazine sold out 20,000 copies within the first two days, and until now, 80,000 copies have been sold, which records the highest number of copies sold since the magazine's start in July 2010.he was announced to have become the first ever male muse of the cosmetics brand Bobbi Brown.He is also Gucci Brand Ambassador.
